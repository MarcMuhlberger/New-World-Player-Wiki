Reaching Higher Levels

Once you have began your journey, and you’ve decided to take your path toward a class, and faction, you may want to consider, “what’s the best way to get good?"

The Community Board

Crafting Quests

Best
Gives you crafting levels and XP. Only requires raw iron + leather.

Supply Quests

Better
Can be expensive but also alot of xp per gold return.

Metalwork Quests

Good
Most expensive, but still high reward.
This is by far the fastest way to gain levels. These offer on average 1000xp per quest and require only money and resources.

The Excavation Sites

If you follow the main storyline, you'll find yourself at the Excavation Sites. These are great for leveling at any level, and people are always looking for members to start one. As long as you find someone with a "orb" (the key to the mine), you can go multiple times.

Amerine Excavation

You can begin at level 19 if you have a good group. Move to next mine once you reach level 30.

Starstone

Level 30 through 42. Don't use the firestaff, most of the creaures are resistant. Best strategy for the final Boss is to space everyone out, and dodge the Boss's AOE attack.

Depths

Level 42 - 60. The final boss will spawn corrupted dogs. Focus these dogs while your tank distracts the Boss. These can break a group apart.
