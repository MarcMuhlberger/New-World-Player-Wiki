Types of Classes

Classes in New World are used not only to define your character's stat specialization but also your own playstyle. Think of the three classes Tank, DPS, and Healer, as personality types. Each player chooses the class which best represents their desired goal with the game.

The Tank

Tanks are what we reference in New world as a member who will be able to pull all of the mobs towards them and absorb all the hits. Hence the name. They are going to have a high constitution and wear heavy armor. Their whole purpose is to be the center of attention for all incoming damage.

The "DPS"

The DPS role or “damage per second” is meant to output the largest damage possible within the group. DPS roles are very important because in unison with a tank they make one strong and effective team. A DPS role will most likely be stacking their skills in accordance with whichever weapon they chose. Maximum damage is the goal.

The Healer

Last but definitely not least is our healer. Healers will keep everyone alive. Stacking most (if not all) of their points into the “Focus” skill, they are meant to keep the tank alive in order for the DPS to not pull any creatures their way. Healers are not going to be frontline players and for most people, this can be boring, however, you are easily the most sought-after people in this game.

In-Game Chat Abbreviations

"LFG"

Looking for group
There is a solo player looking to join a group.

"LFM"

Looking for member
There is a group looking for a player to join.

"LF (class)"

Looking for (spacific class)
There is a group looking for a player with a spacific class type.
Players will want a certain stack of classes when making a group. This can vary player to player as everyone has their preferences so don't get offended if you are rejected on that factor. Players tend to include what class they are to help find a better group dynamic and will specify by usually including their class type before their "LFG" or "LFM" message.
